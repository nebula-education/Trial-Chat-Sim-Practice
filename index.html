Simulation

You've learned the theory. Now it's time to put it into practice — feel the pressure of a real chat, but in a safe space, with no real client on the other side.

The AI Trial Simulator replicates the experience of a real trial chat: a client with a live request, a 3-minute countdown, and the same psychological dynamics you'll face in an actual session.

Choose a client type and start the session. The AI plays the client in real time, with realistic delays and behavioural patterns based on actual user psychology.
You respond the way you would in a live chat — your own tone, your own structure, your own pace.
Send your first message as fast as possible. In a trial chat, every second counts.
Each session runs for exactly 3 minutes. After the timer ends, you'll have additional time to send a final engagement — mirroring the blurred message mechanic in real chats.
📋 Answer Sufficiency
Did your reading actually answer the client's question? Vague phrases like "his energy is complex" score low. A clear, grounded response with at least one specific insight scores high.
💜 Empathy
Do you mirror the client's feelings and name them specifically? Generic phrases like "I understand how you feel" score low. Specific, personal acknowledgement scores high.
⚡ First Response Speed
Your first response must arrive within 7 seconds. In a real trial, slow openings signal absence — and the client may leave before you've said a word.
🔗 Engagement
Do you go deeper than the surface request? Do you ask clarifying questions that open the conversation rather than close it? Strong engagement keeps the client in the chat and builds the reason to return.
✨ Intrigue at the End
Your final message — sent after the timer ends — must create a genuine reason to continue and carry enough value that paying to read it feels worth it.
This module is optional. But the advisors who practise here consistently are the ones who improve fastest. There's no pass or fail — feedback after every chat is only for you.
